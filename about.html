About Tune Tutor

Music learning simplified with professional, one-on-one online classes.

Our Mission

Tune Tutor offers live, one-on-one online music classes taught by professional tutors, catering to students of all ages. Our classes are available in English and major Indian languages, covering a wide range of musical instruments and vocal styles.

Our Music Teachers

Our tutors, spread across major cities in India and international locations, are proficient in English and major Indian languages. Each tutor is meticulously selected through a stringent interview process and holds professional music qualifications.

Key Features

Professional Tutors

Over 75 verified tutors who are qualified and experienced in teaching music.

Global Reach

Serving students in 120+ cities worldwide, including the USA, Canada, UK, Germany, France, UAE, Singapore, India, and more.

Customized Learning

Learn to play your favorite songs in various genres with classes tailored to your individual needs.

Grade Exams Preparation

Preparation for music grade exams conducted by Trinity College London, Rockschool, and ABRSM.

Convenience and Flexibility

Online classes eliminate the need for travel, allowing students to learn at their own pace from the comfort of their homes.

Music Tools Integration

Incorporates tools like a metronome, shruti box, guitar tuner, and vocal pitch monitor for an enhanced learning experience.
